About Me

Hello! My name is Javier, and I am a Street Photographer

Ever since I was young, I’ve been drawn to the beauty of everyday scenes in the city. What began as casual snapshots with my smartphone quickly grew into a deep passion for street photography. Captivated by the raw emotions and stories unfolding on the streets, I started practicing daily, experimenting with angles, lighting, and timing to capture the perfect moment. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills, turning the city into my canvas. Today, I share my unique perspective through my online gallery, where you can explore the essence of urban life through my lens!
